### Acquisition

- **Quest rewards** (primary source) — tier matches quest difficulty
- All custom foods are universal items (safe for any class to use)

### Usage Tips

- **Chicken Stew** is a reliable no-effect meal for pure hunger recovery
- **Elven Bread** has the highest saturation modifier (1.0) despite low hunger — great sustain
- **Phoenix Roast** is the best all-around food: max hunger, max saturation, healing + fire immunity
- **Shadow Cake** is ideal for stealth approaches (Invisibility + Speed)
- **Starfruit Smoothie** is the miner's best friend (Haste II for 45s)
- **Void Truffles** provide the strongest defensive combo (Resistance II + Strength I)
- Save Tier 3 foods for boss fights and dangerous exploration
