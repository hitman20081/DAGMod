# Synergies

When race and class combine, something extraordinary happens. Race-class combinations can unlock unique synergy abilities that go beyond the sum of their parts. These synergies define your character's specialty and playstyle.

**Current Version:** v1.5.4-beta

---

## What Are Synergies?

Synergies are **special passive bonuses** that activate when you combine a specific race with a specific class. They stack with your base race and class bonuses, creating powerful specialized builds.

**Important:** Synergies are **passive abilities** that activate automatically. This is different from **class abilities** (like Rage, Arcane Barrier, Assassinate), which are active abilities you manually use.

**All synergies in DAGMod are passive** and activate based on conditions like location, health level, or combat state.

## Synergy Activation

### How Synergies Work

**Automatic Activation:**
All synergies are passive and activate automatically based on conditions:
- **Location-based:** Underground (Y < 50), Forest biomes
- **Combat-based:** Taking damage, dealing backstab damage, low health (<30%)
- **State-based:** Sneaking in forests

**No Manual Activation Required:**
You don't need to press buttons or use items. Simply play your character and the synergies activate when appropriate.

**Check Frequency:**
Synergies are checked periodically (approximately every 1 second based on game ticks).

### Synergy Conditions

**Underground Check (Dwarf Warrior):**
- Activates when Y-level < 50
- Checked periodically every few seconds
- Grants Resistance effect

**Forest Check (Elf synergies):**
- Checks biome type periodically
- Includes: Forest, Birch Forest, Dark Forest, and all variants
- Elf Mage: Grants Haste
- Elf Rogue: Grants Invisibility (must also be sneaking)

**Health Check (Orc Warrior):**
- Activates below 30% max health
- Continuously monitored during combat
- Grants Strength + Speed

**Damage Check (Human Warrior):**
- Activates when taking damage.
- Grants Absorption I for 10 seconds (200 ticks).
- The effect is not re-applied if it is already active.

**Backstab Check (Orc Rogue):**
- Applies to all attacks from behind
- Stacks with base Rogue backstab bonus
- +20% additional damage multiplier

## Synergies vs. Class Abilities

**Important Distinction:**

**Synergies (Passive):**
- Activate automatically based on conditions
- No cooldowns or manual activation
- Always-on bonuses or conditional effects
- Examples: Dwarf Warrior Resistance underground, Elf Rogue forest invisibility

**Class Abilities (Active):**
- Must be manually activated by using ability items
- Have cooldowns (15-120 seconds)
- Powerful effects you control
- Examples: Battle Shout, Arcane Missiles, Blink Strike
- See [Classes](<./CLASSES/OVERVIEW.md>) page for all 17 new abilities!

**You have BOTH synergies and class abilities** working together!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I have multiple synergies?**
A: No, you have ONE synergy based on your race-class combination.

**Q: Do synergies stack with class abilities?**
A: Yes! Synergies are passive, class abilities are active. They work together.

**Q: Can I change my synergy?**
A: No, synergies are tied to your permanent race and class choices.

**Q: Which synergy is best?**
A: Depends on playstyle! Dwarf Warrior for tanking, Elf Rogue for stealth, Orc Rogue for damage.

**Q: Do all 12 race-class combinations have unique synergies?**
A: No, only 9 combinations have unique synergies. Dwarf Rogue, Elf Warrior, and Orc Mage do not have unique synergies beyond their combined base bonuses.
