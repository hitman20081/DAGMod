## ⚔️ Warrior - The Shield of Steel

**Philosophy:** "Strength and honor above all." Warriors are unstoppable forces on the battlefield, standing firm where others would fall.

### Combat Style
Front-line melee combatant who thrives in close-quarters battle. Warriors are the backbone of any group, drawing enemy attention and dealing devastating melee damage while shrugging off attacks that would kill squishier classes.

### Stat Bonuses
- **Health:** +8 HP (+4 hearts)
- **Melee Damage:** +25% increased melee attack damage
- **Movement Speed:** -10% movement speed

### Warrior Abilities (6 Total)

Warriors command **6 powerful abilities** that combine devastating offense with incredible survivability!

#### 1. Rage
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Duration:** 10 seconds
**Effects:**
- **+50% attack damage** (Strength II)
- **+40% movement speed** (Speed II)
- **+2 hearts absorption**

**Strategy:**
A short burst of incredible power. Use it to finish off a tough enemy or to turn the tide of a fight.

**Best Used:**
- When you need a quick burst of damage.
- To chase down a fleeing enemy.

#### 2. Shield Bash
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Requires:** Shield
**Effects:**
- **Dashes forward 4 blocks**
- **Deals 4 damage (2 hearts)**
- **Knocks back enemies 5 blocks**
- **Stuns enemies with Slowness II for 2 seconds**

**Strategy:**
A great utility ability for creating space and controlling enemies. Can also be used for a short burst of mobility.

**Best Used:**
- To push enemies off cliffs.
- To interrupt enemy spellcasting.
- To get out of a sticky situation.

#### 3. Battle Shout
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Duration:** 12 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Heal 6 hearts (12 HP) immediately**
- **+20% attack damage** for 12 seconds (Strength I)
- **+20% movement speed** for 12 seconds (Speed I)
- **Removes negative effects** (Poison, Weakness, Slowness, Mining Fatigue, Wither)

**Strategy:**
A powerful self-heal and buff. Use it when you're low on health or need an extra edge in a fight.

**Best Used:**
- In the middle of a tough fight to turn the tide.
- To cleanse yourself of nasty debuffs.

#### 4. Whirlwind
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Radius:** 5 blocks
**Effects:**
- **Deals 8 damage (4 hearts)** to all enemies in a 5 block radius.
- **Ignores armor** (true damage)
- **Knocks back enemies**

**Strategy:**
The perfect ability for dealing with large groups of enemies. The true damage makes it effective against armored foes.

**Best Used:**
- When you're surrounded by enemies.
- To clear out a room in a dungeon.

#### 5. Iron Skin
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds (2 minutes)
**Duration:** 15 seconds
**Effects:**
- **+8 absorption hearts (16 HP shield)**
- **Resistance II (60% damage reduction)**
- **Slowness I**
- **Fire Resistance**

**Strategy:**
Your ultimate defensive ability. Use it when you're about to take a massive amount of damage. The slowness is a trade-off for the incredible damage reduction.

**Best Used:**
- When fighting a boss with a powerful attack.
- To survive a fall from a great height.

#### 6. War Cry
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds
**Radius:** 15 blocks
**Effects:**
- **Ally buffs:** Strength I, Resistance I, Speed I for 15 seconds
- **Enemy debuffs:** Slowness I, Weakness I for 8 seconds

**Strategy:**
A powerful AOE buff and debuff. Use it at the start of a big fight to give your team an advantage.

**Best Used:**
- In a team fight to buff your allies and debuff your enemies.
- To make a group of enemies easier to deal with.

### Ability Synergies & Combos

**Offensive Combo:**
1. Whirlwind (AoE damage + crowd control)
2. Battle Shout (Strength I for increased damage)
3. Continue attacking with boosted damage

**Defensive Combo:**
1. Iron Skin (Resistance II)
2. Battle Shout (healing + regeneration)
3. Fight through damage with minimal HP loss

**Boss Fight Rotation:**
1. Start with Battle Shout (damage + healing)
2. Use Whirlwind for AoE if adds spawn
3. Save Iron Skin for when boss enters rage mode
4. Repeat as cooldowns refresh

### Strengths
- Highest survivability with 6 defensive and offensive abilities
- Exceptional melee damage output
- Now has AoE capability (Whirlwind)
- Self-healing (Battle Shout)
- Ultimate tank mode (Iron Skin)
- Great for beginners and aggressive players
- Excels in both solo and group play

### Weaknesses
- Limited mobility compared to Rogue
- No stealth capabilities
- Abilities have moderate to long cooldowns (20-120s)

### Playstyle Tips
- Rush into combat and draw enemy attention
- Use shields to block projectiles
- Manage ability cooldowns - don't waste them all at once
- Battle Standard for healing + damage, Whirlwind for crowds, Iron Talisman for survival
- Stand your ground - you're built to take hits
- Excellent for dungeon diving and boss fights
- In group play, tank for your team

### Best Race Synergies
- **Dwarf Warrior:** Gains Resistance underground (Y<50) + extra HP - unstoppable cave tank
- **Orc Warrior:** Berserker rage (Strength + Speed) when below 30% health - devastating when cornered
- **Human Warrior:** Absorption hearts when taking damage - extra survivability buffer
