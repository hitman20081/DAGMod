# Classes

Master your destiny by choosing one of three powerful classes. Each class offers unique abilities, playstyles, and combat approaches. Combined with your race, classes unlock powerful synergies that define your role in the world.

**Current Version:** v1.5.4-beta features 17 new class abilities bringing devastating power to all three classes!

## Choosing Your Class

### Selection Process
1. Locate the **Hall of Champions**
2. Approach the **Class Selection Altar** (after selecting race)
3. Read the **Class Selection Tome** to study each class
4. Use a **Class Token** at the altar to make your choice
5. **Your choice can be changed with Class reset Potions and Crystal**

### Decision Factors

**Choose Warrior if you:**
- Enjoy close-range melee combat
- Want to be tanky and hard to kill
- Prefer straightforward, aggressive gameplay
- Are new to Minecraft or RPG mods
- Play the tank role in group settings
- Want powerful self-healing and damage

**Choose Mage if you:**
- Prefer ranged magical combat
- Enjoy variety (4 abilities + wands + scrolls)
- Like support and utility roles
- Are comfortable with fragile characters
- Value crowd control and AoE damage
- Want defensive options (Barrier Charm)

**Choose Rogue if you:**
- Love speed and mobility
- Enjoy high-risk, high-reward gameplay
- Want to master dual ability systems
- Prefer hit-and-run tactics and positioning
- Are an experienced player seeking challenge
- Want the highest skill ceiling and versatility

### Class Synergy Considerations
Before choosing your class, review your race selection! Certain race-class combinations unlock powerful synergies:

- **Tank Builds:** Dwarf Warrior, Orc Warrior
- **Magic Builds:** Elf Mage, Dwarf Mage, Human Mage
- **Stealth Builds:** Elf Rogue (invisibility), Orc Rogue (damage)
- **Balanced Builds:** All Human combinations
- **Fast Leveling:** All Human combinations (+25% XP)

## Class Comparison

### Survivability Ranking
1. **Warrior** - 5 abilities including self-healing and tank mode
2. **Mage** - Barrier Charm provides good protection
3. **Rogue** - Speed and escapes, but squishier

### Damage Ranking (Burst)
1. **Rogue** - Highest burst with backstabs + Assassin's Mark
2. **Mage** - High AoE burst with Mana Catalyst
3. **Warrior** - Consistent high damage, boosted by Battle Standard

### Damage Ranking (Sustained)
1. **Warrior** - Consistent melee + abilities
2. **Rogue** - Fast attacks + crits + poison
3. **Mage** - Relies on ability cooldowns

### Utility Ranking
1. **Mage** - Enchanting, brewing, crowd control, 4 abilities + wands + scrolls
2. **Rogue** - 7 abilities, mobility, stealth, night vision
3. **Warrior** - Limited utility outside combat

### Difficulty Ranking
1. **Warrior** - Easy - forgiving, straightforward, self-healing
2. **Mage** - Medium - ability management, positioning, staying alive
3. **Rogue** - Hard - requires dual system mastery, timing, positioning

### Ability Count
1. **Rogue** - 7 abilities (3 energy + 4 cooldown) + passive backstab
2. **Warrior** - 6 abilities
3. **Mage** - 4 abilities + wands + scrolls
