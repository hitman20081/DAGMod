**Philosophy:** "Knowledge is the truest power." Mages wield devastating magical forces, bending reality to their will.

### Combat Style
Ranged spellcaster who dominates from a safe distance. Mages excel at magical combat, crowd control, and both offensive and defensive magic. With 4 permanent abilities plus wands and scrolls, Mages have incredible versatility.

### Stat Bonuses
- **Health:** +2 HP (+1 heart)
- **Melee Damage:** -25% melee attack damage
- **Enchantment Costs:** 50% reduced XP costs at enchanting tables
- **Potion Duration:** +50% longer lasting potion effects
- **Permanent Night Vision:** See in the dark at all times

### Mage Abilities (4 Total)

Mages command **4 powerful abilities** that manipulate arcane energies.

#### 1. Arcane Barrier
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Duration:** 10 seconds
**Effects:**
- **+10 absorption hearts (20 HP shield)**
- **Resistance II (40% damage reduction)**
- **Fire Resistance**
- **Reflects projectiles (Thorns-like effect)**

**Strategy:**
A powerful defensive spell that can turn the tide of a battle. Use it when you are about to take a lot of damage.

**Best Used:**
- When fighting a boss with a powerful attack.
- To survive a fall from a great height.

#### 2. Arcane Missiles
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Fires 5 homing arcane missiles.**
- **Each missile deals 3 damage (1.5 hearts).**
- **Total: 15 damage (7.5 hearts) if all hit.**

**Strategy:**
A reliable source of damage. Use it to pick off enemies from a distance.

**Best Used:**
- To finish off a fleeing enemy.
- To get some chip damage in before a fight.

#### 3. Mana Burst
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Radius:** 7 blocks
**Effects:**
- **Deals 10 damage (5 hearts)** to all enemies in a 7 block radius.
- **Ignores armor** (true damage)
- **Knocks back enemies.**

**Strategy:**
A powerful AoE spell that is great for clearing out groups of enemies.

**Best Used:**
- When you are surrounded by enemies.
- To clear out a room in a dungeon.

#### 4. Time Warp
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Radius:** 10 blocks
**Duration:** 8 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Slowness IV (80% slower)** on enemies.
- **Weakness II** on enemies.
- **Mining Fatigue III** on enemies.

**Strategy:**
A powerful crowd control spell that can be used to escape from a sticky situation or to set up a combo.

**Best Used:**
- When you are being chased by a large group of enemies.
- To give yourself time to heal or cast other spells.

### Mage Equipment Systems

**Wands:**
Mages can use wands to cast spells. There are three tiers of wands:
- **Apprentice Wand:** Mana Cost: 15, Charge Time: 2.5s, Effect: Launches a small fireball.
- **Adept Wand:** Mana Cost: 12, Charge Time: 2.0s, Effect: Launches a regular fireball.
- **Master Wand:** Mana Cost: 10, Charge Time: 1.5s, Effect: Launches a large fireball (ghast fireball).

**Spell Scrolls:**
Single-use scrolls that cast powerful spells. Scrolls can be crafted or obtained through Mage-specific quests.
- **Heal Scroll:** Heals the caster.
- **Absorption Scroll:** Grants absorption hearts.
- **Mana Shield Scroll:** Creates a shield that absorbs damage using mana.
- **Fireball Scroll:** Launches a fireball.
- **Teleport Scroll:** Teleports the caster a short distance.
- **Lightning Scroll:** Summons a lightning bolt at the target location.
- **Frost Nova Scroll:** Creates a nova of ice that slows and damages enemies.

### Ability Combos

**Defensive Setup:**
1. Arcane Barrier (10 absorption hearts before fight)
2. Enter combat with protection active

**Crowd Control Combo:**
1. Time Warp (slow all enemies)
2. Arcane Missiles (hit slowed targets easily)
3. Mana Burst (AoE damage while they're slow)

**Room Clearing:**
1. Time Warp (slow everything)
2. Mana Burst (massive explosion)
3. Arcane Missiles (finish survivors)

**Emergency Escape:**
1. Time Warp (slow pursuers)
2. Arcane Barrier if needed (protection while escaping)
3. Run while enemies are slowed

### Strengths
- 4 permanent abilities provide incredible versatility
- Devastating magical damage (Mana Burst, Arcane Missiles)
- Excellent crowd control (Time Warp)
- Strong defensive option (Arcane Barrier)
- Superior enchanting and brewing capabilities
- Long-range combat keeps you safe
- Permanent night vision for cave exploration
- Additional utility from wands and scrolls

### Weaknesses
- Fragile - low health pool
- Terrible at melee combat
- Must manage ability cooldowns carefully
- Vulnerable if enemies get close (before Arcane Barrier)
- Abilities have moderate cooldowns (20-60s)

### Playstyle Tips
- Keep distance from enemies at all costs
- Use Barrier Charm proactively (before danger, not after)
- Temporal Crystal is your panic button - use when overwhelmed
- Focus on enchanting gear early for maximum power
- Brew potions constantly - they're your lifeline
- Position carefully in combat - stay at range
- Arcane Orb on cooldown for consistent damage
- Excellent for base building and support roles
- Master enchanting to gear up your team

### Best Race Synergies
- **Dwarf Mage:** Permanent Fire Resistance - safe Nether exploration
- **Elf Mage:** Haste in forest biomes - fastest caster in game
- **Human Mage:** Random regeneration bursts - helps with fragility
