### Lore
Elves are the eldest of races, guardians of the ancient forests who move with supernatural grace. Their deep connection to nature allows them to coax more bounty from the land, and their keen senses make them unmatched scouts. They are friends to all growing things, and the forest itself seems to bend to aid them.
