### Lore
Orcs are born warriors, raised in harsh lands where only the strong survive. Their incredible strength and endurance make them fearsome in battle, and their hunting prowess ensures they never go hungry. What they lack in subtlety, they make up for in raw, overwhelming power.

### Synergies
- **Orc Warrior:** Berserker rage (Strength + Speed) when below 30% health - most dangerous when wounded
- **Orc Mage:** No unique synergy - natural combination of Orc durability + Mage casting
- **Orc Rogue:** +20% extra backstab damage (stacks with base Rogue backstab) - highest burst damage in game
