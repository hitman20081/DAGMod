## Race-Specific Quest Chains

Each race has an **epic 10-quest storyline** that progresses through all 4 difficulty tiers. These quests are available starting at character creation and tell a complete story of your race's journey.

### Quest Chain Details

**Total Race-Specific Quests:** 40 (10 per race)

**Requirements:**
- Must have selected the appropriate race
- Quests unlock at proper level thresholds (1, 5, 15, 25)
- Progress through tiers: Novice → Apprentice → Expert → Master

**XP Rewards:**
- Novice: 200 XP
- Apprentice: 500 XP
- Expert: 1500 XP
- Master: 2500 XP

**Total XP from Race Chain:** 11,700 XP (significant progression boost!)

**Epic Rewards:**
Each race chain provides unique legendary rewards fitting their theme:
- **Dwarf:** Full Netherite gear, Nether Star, smithing mastery
- **Elf:** Elytra, Totems of Undying, nature mastery
- **Human:** Full Netherite set, Dragon Egg, legendary status
- **Orc:** Full Netherite combat gear, Dragon Egg, boss trophies

## Choosing Your Race

### Selection Process
1. Locate the **Hall of Champions** (use Hall Locator item or explore naturally)
2. Approach the **Race Selection Altar**
3. Read the **Race Selection Tome** to learn about each option
4. Use a **Race Token** at the altar to make your choice
5. **Your choice is permanent** - choose wisely!

### Considerations
- **Playstyle:** Do you prefer combat, exploration, or resource gathering?
- **Class Synergy:** Which class do you plan to choose? Check synergy bonuses!
- **Quest Chain:** Which epic storyline appeals to you?
- **Environment:** Where will you spend most of your time?
- **Team Composition:** Playing multiplayer? Coordinate with teammates!
- **Progression Speed:** Want to level faster? Choose Human for +25% XP

### Can I Change My Race?
**No.** Race selection is permanent. Choose wisely!

## Race Comparison

### Best for Leveling Speed
1. **Human** - +25% XP gain = fastest to level 50
2. Orc - No XP bonus
3. Dwarf - No XP bonus
4. Elf - No XP bonus

### Best for Survivability
1. **Orc** - 23 HP (highest health)
2. **Dwarf** - 21 HP + underground Resistance
3. Human/Elf - 20 HP (standard)

### Best for Resource Gathering
1. **Dwarf** - Mining speed + ore bonuses (best early-game)
2. **Elf** - Wood/leaf bonuses + Hero of Village trades
3. **Orc** - Hunting bonuses for food/leather
4. Human - No gathering bonuses

### Best for Mobility
1. **Elf** - +15% movement speed + extended reach
2. Human/Orc/Dwarf - Standard speed
3. Dwarf - -5% movement speed (slowest)

### Best for Combat
1. **Orc** - Highest HP + melee damage bonus
2. **Dwarf** - Extra HP for durability
3. Human/Elf - Standard combat capabilities
