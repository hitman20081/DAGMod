## ⛏️ Dwarf - The Mountainborn

**Philosophy:** Hardy miners and underground specialists. Masters of stone, metal, and the deep places of the world.

### Stat Bonuses
- **Health:** 22 HP (11 hearts) - **+2 HP (1 heart) bonus**
- **Movement Speed:** -5% (slightly slower)
- Increased durability and resilience

### Gathering Bonuses
- **Mining Speed:** +20% faster when using pickaxes
- **Ore Drops:** 15% chance for bonus ore drops (double drops when triggered)
  - Works on: Coal, Iron, Gold, Diamond, Emerald, Redstone, Lapis
  - Significantly accelerates resource gathering

### Special Traits
- Born for the underground
- Natural resistance to mining hazards
- Expert resource gatherers
- Best early-game progression through mining bonuses

### Race Quest Chain: "The Forgemaster's Legacy"
**10-Quest Epic Storyline** - Journey from Apprentice to Forgemaster

**Theme:** Mining, crafting, and mastery of underground realms

**Journey:**
- Begin as a mining Apprentice
- Master the forge and ancient smithing techniques
- Explore the deepest caverns and Nether
- Become a legendary Forgemaster

**Quest Progression:** Novice → Apprentice → Expert → Master tiers

**Rewards:**
- Full Netherite gear with enchantments
- Nether Star
- Legendary smithing items
- Forgemaster title and mastery

### Lore
Dwarves are children of the mountains, born in the depths where stone meets molten core. Their connection to earth and metal is unmatched, and their stout frames can withstand blows that would fell lesser beings. They see in the dark better than most, and can sense the presence of precious ores through solid rock.

### Synergies
- **Dwarf Warrior:** Gains Resistance effect when underground (below Y=50) - unstoppable cave tank
- **Dwarf Mage:** Permanent Fire Resistance from mountain forge heritage - Nether master
- **Dwarf Rogue:** No unique synergy - natural combination of Dwarf mining + Rogue stealth

### Strategy Tips
- Best race for early-game progression through mining
- Bonus ore drops accelerate gear advancement significantly
- Slightly slower movement is offset by extra durability
- Build bases near mining operations
- Excellent for resource-focused playstyles
- Complete "The Forgemaster's Legacy" for ultimate mining rewards
- Dwarf Warrior is nearly unkillable underground (Resistance + extra HP)
