# Weapons & Shields

DAGMod features 15 special weapons, 5 Mythril tools, 9 themed shields, and the Solar Bow. All custom weapons use the Mythril tool material (2500 durability) and come with auto-applied enchantments when first equipped.

### Weapon Highlights

- **Fastest weapons:** Shadowfang Dagger (-1.6), Gilded Rapier / Phantom Blade (-1.8) -- great for Rogues
- **Highest damage:** True King Sword (17.0), Crystalhammer (17.0), Thunder Pike / Ethereal Blade / Frostbite Axe (16.0)
- **Best looting:** Bloodthirster Blade (Sharpness V + Looting III) -- the ultimate farming weapon
- **Best ranged:** Solar Bow (Power V + Flame III + Infinity)
- **True King Sword** has Sharpness X and Smite X -- the most powerful weapon in the mod

## Armor Set Weapon Synergies

Equipping a matching weapon with 2+ pieces of its armor set grants bonus effects:

| Armor Set | Matching Weapon | 2-Piece Synergy | 4-Piece Synergy |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Dragonscale | Dragonscale Sword | Strength I, +15% damage | Strength II, Fire Resistance, +30% damage |
| Inferno | Inferno Sword | Strength I, +15% damage | Strength II, Haste II, +25% damage |
| Shadow | Shadowfang Sword / Phantom Blade | Speed II (+Invis for Rogue) | Speed III, Jump Boost III (+75% backstab for Rogue) |
| Nature's Guard | Poison Fang Spear | Regen II, Luck I | Regen III, Luck II, Saturation |
| Crystalforge | Crystalhammer | Absorption II, Resistance I | Absorption III, Resistance II, Jump Boost III |

## Acquisition

- **Crafting** - Most weapons and shields have crafting recipes
- **Quest Rewards** - Higher tier quests award better weapons
- **Boss Drops** - Bone Realm bosses can drop epic gear
- **Enchantsmith NPC** - May stock enchanted weapons in rotating trades
