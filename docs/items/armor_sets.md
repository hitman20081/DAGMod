# Armor Sets ‚≠ê 6 Custom Sets

**Progressive armor sets with unique bonuses and synergies!**

**Features:**
- Each set has 4 pieces (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots)
- Progressive bonuses (more pieces = stronger effects)
- Class-specific benefits
- Synergy with weapons
- Set bonuses stack with race/class bonuses

**Acquisition:** Quest rewards, crafting, boss drops.

### Dragonscale Armor
**Theme:** Dragonic power and resilience.
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Class-specific (Warrior: Resistance, Mage: Glowing, Rogue: Speed)
- **4-Piece Bonus:** Class-specific (Warrior: Resistance II, Mage: Mana Regen, Rogue: Speed II)
- **Weapon Synergy:** Dragonscale Blade (Enhances fire damage, chance to ignite ground)

### Crystalforge Armor
**Theme:** Mystical energy and sharpened senses.
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Night Vision), Class-specific (Mage: Regeneration, Warrior: Absorption, Rogue: Jump Boost)
- **4-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Glowing), Class-specific (Mage: -20% mana costs + Regen II, Warrior: Absorption II, Rogue: Jump Boost II)
- **Weapon Synergy:** Crystal Hammer (Enhanced density damage, shockwaves on smash attacks)

### Inferno Armor
**Theme:** Fiery fury and heat resistance.
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Fire Resistance), Class-specific (Warrior: Strength, Mage: Regeneration, Rogue: Speed)
- **4-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Fire Resistance II + Glowing), Class-specific (Warrior: Strength II, Mage: Regeneration II, Rogue: Haste II)
- **Weapon Synergy:** Inferno Blade (Enhanced fire damage, burning aura)

### Nature's Guard Armor
**Theme:** Harmony with nature and regenerative power.
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Regeneration), Class-specific (Warrior: Resistance, Mage: Luck, Rogue: Jump Boost II)
- **4-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Regeneration II + Saturation), Class-specific (Warrior: Absorption II, Mage: Luck II, Rogue: Invisibility)
- **Weapon Synergy:** Poison Fang Spear (Poison enhancement, poison spreads, heal on poisoned kill)

### Shadow Armor
**Theme:** Stealth, speed, and dark magic.
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Speed + Night Vision), Rogue-specific (Invisibility)
- **4-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Speed II + Night Vision), Class-specific (Warrior: Resistance, Mage: Teleport Power, Rogue: Invisibility + Jump Boost II)
- **Weapon Synergy:** Shadow Blade or Phantom Blade (Enhanced stealth attacks, increased backstab damage, teleport on kill)

### Fortuna Armor
**Theme:** Luck and fortune.
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Luck III + Speed)
- **4-Piece Bonus:** Universal (Luck V + Hero of Village + Speed II)
- **No specific weapon synergy.**
