# Warrior Ability Items (6)

Warriors command **6 powerful abilities** that combine devastating offense with incredible survivability!

#### 1. Rage Totem
**Ability:** Rage / Temporary Combat Enhancement
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Duration:** 10 seconds
**Effects:**
- **+50% attack damage** (Strength II)
- **+40% movement speed** (Speed II)
- **+2 hearts absorption**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A short burst of incredible power.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 2. Shield Bash
**Ability:** Shield Bash / Dash and Knockback
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Requires:** Shield
**Effects:**
- **Dashes forward 4 blocks**
- **Deals 4 damage (2 hearts)**
- **Knocks back enemies 5 blocks**
- **Stuns enemies with Slowness II for 2 seconds**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Great for creating space and controlling enemies.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 3. Battle Horn
**Ability:** Battle Shout / Self-Buff
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Duration:** 12 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Heal 6 hearts (12 HP) immediately**
- **+20% attack damage** for 12 seconds (Strength I)
- **+20% movement speed** for 12 seconds (Speed I)
- **Removes negative effects** (Poison, Weakness, Slowness, Mining Fatigue, Wither)

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A powerful self-heal and buff.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 4. Whirlwind Axe
**Ability:** Whirlwind / AoE Spin Attack
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Radius:** 5 blocks
**Effects:**
- **Deals 8 damage (4 hearts)** to all enemies in a 5 block radius.
- **Ignores armor** (true damage)
- **Knocks back enemies**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Perfect for dealing with large groups of enemies.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 5. Iron Skin Talisman
**Ability:** Iron Skin / Defensive Tank Mode
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds (2 minutes)
**Duration:** 15 seconds
**Effects:**
- **+8 absorption hearts (16 HP shield)**
- **Resistance II (40% damage reduction)**
- **Slowness I**
- **Fire Resistance**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Your ultimate defensive ability.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 6. War Cry Banner
**Ability:** War Cry / AOE Buff and Debuff
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds
**Radius:** 15 blocks
**Effects:**
- **Ally buffs:** Strength I, Resistance I, Speed I for 15 seconds
- **Enemy debuffs:** Slowness I, Weakness I for 8 seconds

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A powerful AOE buff and debuff.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.
