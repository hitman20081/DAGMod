# Mage Ability Items (4)

Mages command **4 powerful abilities** that manipulate arcane energies.

#### 1. Arcane Barrier
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Duration:** 10 seconds
**Effects:**
- **+10 absorption hearts (20 HP shield)**
- **Resistance II (40% damage reduction)**
- **Fire Resistance**
- **Reflects projectiles (Thorns-like effect)**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A powerful defensive spell.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 2. Arcane Missiles
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Fires 5 homing arcane missiles.**
- **Each missile deals 3 damage (1.5 hearts).**
- **Total: 15 damage (7.5 hearts) if all hit.**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A reliable source of damage.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 3. Mana Burst
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Radius:** 7 blocks
**Effects:**
- **Deals 10 damage (5 hearts)** to all enemies in a 7 block radius.
- **Ignores armor** (true damage)
- **Knocks back enemies.**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A powerful AoE spell.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 4. Time Warp
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Radius:** 10 blocks
**Duration:** 8 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Slowness IV (80% slower)** on enemies.
- **Weakness II** on enemies.
- **Mining Fatigue III** on enemies.

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. A powerful crowd control spell.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.
