# Rogue Ability Items (7 Total)

Rogues uniquely have **TWO separate ability systems** for unmatched versatility!

### SYSTEM 1: Energy-Based Abilities (Rogue Ability Tome)

**Special item that unlocks Rogue's energy-based ability system.**

#### Rogue Ability Tome
**Type:** Permanent ability unlock  
**System:** Energy-based (not cooldowns)

**Unlocks 3 Energy Abilities:**
1. **Smoke Bomb**
**Energy Cost:** 40
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Effects:** Invisibility for 6 seconds, Speed II for 6 seconds, creates a smoke cloud that blinds enemies.

2. **Poison Dagger**
**Energy Cost:** 25
**Duration:** 5 seconds
**Effect:** The next attack applies Poison III for 8 seconds, Weakness II for 6 seconds, and Nausea for 5 seconds.

3. **Shadow Step**
**Energy Cost:** 50
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Effect:** Teleports the player to the location they are looking at (up to 25 blocks away), leaving behind a decoy. Grants invisibility for 3 seconds and Resistance V for 0.5 seconds.

**Features:**
- Energy resource pool (not cooldowns)
- Visual HUD shows energy
- Energy regenerates over time
- Cycle between 3 abilities

**Acquisition:** Quest chain "Path of Shadows"

---

### SYSTEM 2: Cooldown-Based Abilities (Individual Items)

**How It Works:**
- Individual ability items
- Each has its own cooldown
- Use by right-clicking the item
- Works independently from energy system

**Cooldown-Based Abilities (4):**

#### 1. Assassinate
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Deals 20 damage (10 hearts)** when attacking from behind.
- **Deals 10 damage (5 hearts)** from the front as backup.

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Complements passive backstab for insane burst damage.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 2. Blink Strike
**Ability:** Blink Strike / Teleport
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Teleports behind the nearest enemy within 15 blocks.**
- **Grants brief invisibility (2 seconds) and speed boost (3 seconds).**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Perfect engaging tool for surprise backstabs.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 3. Poison Strike
**Ability:** Poison Strike / Debuff
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Applies Poison IV for 8 seconds.**
- **Also applies Weakness II and Slowness I.**
- **Deals 4 initial damage.**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Excellent for tough single targets.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

#### 4. Vanish
**Ability:** Vanish / Ultimate Escape
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds
**Effects:**
- **Invisibility for 8 seconds.**
- **Speed II for 8 seconds.**
- **Blinds nearby enemies for 4 seconds.**

**Usage:** Right-click to activate. Your "oh shit" button when overwhelmed.

**Acquisition:** Quest reward, crafting.

---

**Total Rogue Abilities:** 7 (3 energy + 4 cooldown) + passive backstab = Ultimate versatility!
