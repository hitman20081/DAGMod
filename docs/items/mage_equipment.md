# Mage Equipment

### Spell Scrolls

Single-use scrolls that cast powerful spells.

**Existing Spell Scrolls:**
- **Heal Scroll:** Restores health.
- **Absorption Scroll:** Grants temporary absorption hearts.
- **Mana Shield Scroll:** Creates a protective mana shield.
- **Fireball Scroll:** Launches a fiery projectile.
- **Lightning Scroll:** Summons a lightning bolt.
- **Frost Nova Scroll:** Creates a burst of frost, slowing enemies.
- **Teleport Scroll:** Teleports the user to a target location.

**Acquisition:**
- Mage-specific quest rewards
- Quest chain completion
- Cannot be crafted (as of v1.5.0-beta)

**Usage:**
- Right-click to cast spell
- Scroll is consumed on use
- Effects vary by scroll type

### Wands

Wands are reusable Mage weapons that can cast spells with mana costs and cooldowns.

**Wand Types:**
- **Apprentice Wand:** Early-game Mage weapon.
- **Adept Wand:** Mid-game Mage weapon.
- **Master Wand:** Late-game Mage weapon.

**Features:**
- Reusable, with mana costs and cooldowns for spells.
- Each wand type offers increasing power and potentially different spell effects.
- Mana cost and cooldowns are managed by the Mage class system.

**Acquisition:**
- Quest rewards
- Crafting
- Boss drops
