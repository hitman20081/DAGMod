# Tomes & Information Items

Tomes provide lore, information, and guidance about DAGMod's systems.

### Selection Tomes

| Tome | Purpose | Information Provided |
|------|---------|---------------------|
| Race Selection Tome | Race information | Detailed stats and lore for all 4 races |
| Class Selection Tome | Class information | Abilities and playstyles for all 3 classes |

**Acquisition:**
- Obtained from selection altars
- Read before making your permanent choice
- Provides complete information to inform decisions
