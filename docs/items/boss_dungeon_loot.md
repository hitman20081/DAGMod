### Boss System Loot (v1.4.2-beta)

**Skeleton King Hierarchy:**

**Skeleton King** (Epic Boss):
- **Drops:** Skeleton King's Key (100%), Necrotic Key (5%), Potions (Long Strength, Strong Regeneration), Enchanted Golden Apples, Netherite Ingots, Netherite Blocks, Enchanted Netherite Armor (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots), Enchanted Netherite Swords, Enchanted Books (various), Experience Bottles, Golden Carrots.
- Epic loot befitting the realm's ruler.
- Locked treasure chests requiring keys.

**Skeleton Lord** (Mini-Boss):
- **Drops:** Potions (Long Strength, Strong Regeneration), Enchanted Golden Apples, Diamonds, Diamond Blocks, Enchanted Diamond Armor (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots), Enchanted Diamond Swords, Enchanted Books (various), Experience Bottles.
- Better than minions, not as good as King.

**Field Captain** (Elite):
- **Drops:** Potions (Long Strength, Strong Regeneration), Enchanted Golden Apples, Diamonds, Diamond Blocks, Enchanted Diamond Armor (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots), Enchanted Diamond Swords, Enchanted Books (various), Experience Bottles.
- Elite-tier loot.

**Boneling** (Minion):
- **Drops:** Enchanted Golden Apples, Enchanted Iron Swords, Iron Blocks, Enchanted Iron Armor (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots), Experience Bottles, Golden Carrots, Iron Ingots.
- Basic drops.

**Locked Treasure Chests:**
- **Contents:** Potions (Long Strength, Strong Regeneration), Enchanted Golden Apples, Diamonds, Diamond Blocks, Enchanted Diamond Armor (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots), Enchanted Diamond Swords, Enchanted Books (various), Experience Bottles.
- Require keys from bosses.
- Epic rewards for challenging bosses.

### Bone Dungeon Loot (v1.4.3-beta)

**Treasure Room Chests:**
- Enchanted books (various levels)
- Diamonds (multiple)
- Emeralds
- Golden apples
- Rare gear and equipment
- Best loot in dungeons

**Portal Room Chests:**
- Ender pearls (multiple)
- Obsidian
- Eyes of ender
- Portal-themed items
- Nether/End preparation items

**Regular Room Chests:**
- Bones
- Arrows
- Iron ingots/ore
- Gold ingots/ore
- Basic supplies and materials
- Standard dungeon loot

**Acquisition:** Explore Bone Dungeons (generate naturally underground)
