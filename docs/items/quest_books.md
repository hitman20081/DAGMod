# Quest Books

Quest Books track your available quests and progression through DAGMod's quest tiers.

### Quest Book Tiers (4 Total)

| Book | Tier | Level Required | XP per Quest |
|------|------|----------------|--------------|
| Novice Quest Book | 1 | Level 1 | 200 XP |
| Apprentice Quest Book | 2 | Level 5 | 500 XP |
| Expert Quest Book | 3 | Level 15 | 1500 XP |
| Master Quest Book | 4 | Level 25 | 2500 XP |

**Usage:**
- Interact with Quest Blocks to view available quests
- Shows quest objectives and progress
- Displays rewards for completion
- Cannot access higher-tier quests without meeting level requirements

**Progression:**
- Complete quests in your current tier
- Reach required level for next tier
- Unlock access to higher difficulty quests
