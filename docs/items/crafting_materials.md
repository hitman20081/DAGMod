# Crafting Materials

Resources used to create items and progress through crafting systems.

### Custom Powders (Crafting Materials)

These powders are distinct from consumable powders and are primarily used as crafting ingredients for various items.

-   **Amethyst Powder:** Used in crafting.
-   **Diamond Powder:** Used in crafting.
-   **Emerald Powder:** Used in crafting.
-   **Echo Dust:** Used in crafting.
-   **Quartz Powder:** Used in crafting.
-   **Slimeball Dust:** Used in crafting.
-   **Suspicious Substance:** Used in crafting.

**Uses:** Primarily as ingredients in crafting recipes for advanced items, equipment, and other custom mod content.

### Quest Materials

Items required for quest completion or obtained as quest rewards.

**Common Materials:**
- Various vanilla Minecraft items
- Custom tokens and certificates
- Proof of completion items
