# Fantasy Foods

Restore hunger and gain beneficial effects with DAGMod's themed food items.

### Basic Foods

| Food | Hunger Restored | Special Effect | Theme |
|------|----------------|----------------|-------|
| Elven Bread | 6 (3 drumsticks) | Good saturation | Elven specialty |
| Dwarven Ale | 4 (2 drumsticks) | Brief Strength | Dwarven specialty |
| Orc Ration | 8 (4 drumsticks) | High saturation | Orc specialty |
| Mystic Berry | 3 (1.5 drumsticks) | Brief Regeneration | Magical fruit |
| Honey Cake | 5 (2.5 drumsticks) | Medium saturation | Sweet treat |

**Acquisition:**
- Quest rewards
- Crafting recipes
- Trading
