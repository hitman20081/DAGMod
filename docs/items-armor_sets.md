# Armor Sets - 11 Custom Sets

DAGMod features 11 custom armor sets, each with 4 pieces (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots). Six sets have progressive set bonuses with class-specific effects and weapon synergies. All sets have auto-applied enchantments on first equip.

**Acquisition:** Quest rewards, crafting, boss drops.

## Sets with Bonuses (6 Sets)

### Dragonscale Armor
**Theme:** Dragonic power and resilience. The strongest armor in the game.
- **2-Piece:** Warrior: Resistance I | Mage: -15% spell costs | Rogue: Speed I, +20% crit chance
- **4-Piece:** Warrior: Resistance II | Mage: +50% mana regen | Rogue: Speed II, +25% energy regen
- **Weapon Synergy (Dragonscale Sword):** 2pc: Strength I, +15% damage | 4pc: Strength II, Fire Resistance, +30% damage

### Crystalforge Armor
**Theme:** Mystical energy and sharpened senses.
- **2-Piece:** Night Vision + (Warrior: Absorption I | Mage: Regen I, +25% mana regen | Rogue: Jump Boost I, +15% energy regen)
- **4-Piece:** Glowing + (Warrior: Absorption II | Mage: -20% spell costs, Regen II | Rogue: Jump Boost II)
- **Weapon Synergy (Crystalhammer):** 2pc: Absorption II, Resistance I | 4pc: Absorption III, Resistance II, Jump Boost III

### Inferno Armor
**Theme:** Fiery fury and heat resistance.
- **2-Piece:** Fire Resistance + (Warrior: Strength I | Mage: Regen I | Rogue: Speed I)
- **4-Piece:** Fire Resistance + Glowing + (Warrior: Strength II | Mage: Regen II, -10% spell costs | Rogue: Haste II)
- **Weapon Synergy (Inferno Sword):** 2pc: Strength I, +15% damage | 4pc: Strength II, Haste II, +25% damage

### Nature's Guard Armor
**Theme:** Harmony with nature and regenerative power.
- **2-Piece:** Regen I + (Warrior: Resistance I | Mage: Luck I | Rogue: Jump Boost II)
- **4-Piece:** Regen II, Saturation + (Warrior: Absorption II | Mage: Luck II, +30% mana regen | Rogue: Invisibility, +15% crit)
- **Weapon Synergy (Poison Fang Spear):** 2pc: Regen II, Luck I | 4pc: Regen III, Luck II, Saturation

### Shadow Armor
**Theme:** Stealth, speed, and dark magic. Best for Rogues.
- **2-Piece:** Speed I, Night Vision + (Rogue: Invisibility, +25% crit | Mage: -10% spell costs)
- **4-Piece:** Speed II, Night Vision + (Warrior: Resistance I | Rogue: Invisibility, Jump Boost II, +35% energy regen)
- **Weapon Synergy (Shadowfang Sword / Phantom Blade):** 2pc: Speed II, +35% backstab (Rogue) | 4pc: Speed III, Jump Boost III, +75% backstab (Rogue)

### Fortuna Armor
**Theme:** Luck and fortune.
- **2-Piece:** Luck III + Speed I
- **4-Piece:** Luck V + Hero of the Village + Speed II
- **No weapon synergy.** Pure luck-based set for trading and looting.

## Sets Without Bonuses (5 Sets)

These sets have full armor stats and auto-enchantments but do not have set bonus effects.

### Mythril Armor
**Theme:** Balanced entry-level custom armor. Comparable to Diamond/Netherite.
- Solid all-around stats with Protection III and Unbreaking III
- Good enchantability (18) for additional enchanting

### Frostbound Armor
**Theme:** Ice and cold resistance.
- Boots auto-enchanted with Frost Walker II -- walk on water
- Good durability (42x multiplier) and moderate toughness

### Solarweave Armor
**Theme:** Solar energy and self-repair.
- **Solar Mending:** This set passively repairs itself in direct sunlight. Every 5 seconds, each worn piece regains 1 durability point. Requires daytime, clear skies, and sky visibility (no roof above). The only armor set with self-repair.

### Stormcaller Armor
**Theme:** Storm and lightning resistance.
- Protection III + Projectile Protection II -- strong against ranged attacks
- Good for exploration and ranged combat encounters

### Obsidian Armor
**Theme:** Ultimate tank armor.
- Second-highest protection stats (5/10/8/5)
- Highest toughness after Dragonscale (4.5) and best knockback resistance (0.25)
- Protection IV + Blast Protection III + Unbreaking IV -- extremely durable
- Chestplate has Thorns III for damage reflection
