**DAG Mod** is a lightweight Fabric mod that extends the functionality of datapacks by adding support for crafting recipes and custom items that datapacks alone can’t handle.

This mod allows server owners and modpack creators to integrate custom items into Minecraft using a simple and modular approach. It’s ideal for expanding vanilla-style gameplay while keeping things compatible with Fabric and datapack systems.

---

### What it adds:
- Custom items such as **Suspicious Substance**, **Amethyst Powder**, and **Quartz Powder**
- A custom potion: **Potion of Clarity**, crafted using Amethyst Powder and Quartz Powder
- The ability to add crafting recipes for datapack-defined items
- A framework for adding more advanced recipes and mechanics in future updates

---

### Why use DAG Mod:
- Enables full functionality for custom datapack items, including crafting recipes
- Lightweight and modular design for easy integration
- Perfect for custom adventures, RPG servers, and expanding vanilla mechanics

---

### Important Information:
- 🟩 **Minecraft Version:** 1.21.4  
- 🧩 **Requires Fabric API**  
- 🔁 **Must be installed on both client and server**  
- ❌ Not compatible with Forge  

---

More items, potions, and features are planned for future updates.
