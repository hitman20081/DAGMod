**DAG Mod** is a lightweight Fabric mod that extends the functionality of datapacks by adding support for crafting recipes and custom items that datapacks alone can’t handle.

---

### What it adds:
- **Custom items:**
  - **Suspicious Substance** – A mysterious material with unknown potential
  - **Amethyst Powder** – Finely crushed crystal, used in advanced crafting recipes
  - **Quartz Powder** – Refined Nether quartz, used in specialized crafting recipes
  - **Diamond Powder** – Finely ground diamond, intended for advanced recipes
  - **Emerald Powder** – Crushed emerald, used for special alchemical crafting

- **Custom potions:**
  - 🧪 **Potion of Clarity** – Crafted using Amethyst and Quartz Powder; enhances mental focus or perception (about 5 min)
  - 🧪 **Potion of the Supes** – Grants powerful abilities for a limited long time (30 Min)
  - 🧪 **Essence of Thanos** – A potent and rare potion with dramatic effects (intended for high-tier gameplay) lasting 10 min.

- Adds crafting recipe support for items introduced via datapacks
- Provides a modular framework for expanding with more potions, items, and recipes in future updates

---

### Why use DAG Mod:
- Enables full functionality for custom datapack items, including crafting support
- Adds unique and lore-friendly items and potions without breaking vanilla balance
- Lightweight and modular for easy modpack integration
- Ideal for custom adventures, RPG-style servers, or enhanced survival experiences

---

### Important Information:
- 🟩 **Minecraft Version:** 1.21.4  
- 🧩 **Requires Fabric API**  
- 🔁 **Must be installed on both client and server**  
- ❌ Not compatible with Forge  

---

More items, potions, and features are planned for future updates.
