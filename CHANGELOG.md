# 📦 DAG Mod - Changelog
**Version:** `v1.0.1`  
**Minecraft Version:** `1.21.4`  
**Requirement:** Must be installed on both **server** and **client**

### Added
- 🧱 **Diamond Powder** – Finely ground diamond, used in advanced crafting recipes
- 💎 **Emerald Powder** – Crushed emerald, useful for specialized alchemical crafting
- ✨ **Essence of Thanos** – A powerful potion with dramatic effects
- 💥 **Potion of the Supes** – Grants powerful temporary abilities
- 🧠 **Potion of Clarity** – Enhances focus and perception

### Changed
- Updated descriptions for **Amethyst Powder** and **Quartz Powder**:
  - Both are now described as crafting materials, not potion ingredients

### Notes
- All powders are intended for use in **crafting recipes**, not with brewing stands
- More items, potions, and crafting recipes coming soon!

---

📦 **Requires Fabric API**  
🔁 **Must be installed on both client and server**  
🟩 **Compatible with Minecraft 1.21.4**

# 📦 DAG Mod - Changelog
**Version:** `v1.0.0`  
**Minecraft Version:** `1.21.4`  
**Requirement:** Must be installed on both **server** and **client**

---

## 🆕 Added Items
- **🧪 Suspicious Substance**  
  A strange ingredient with mysterious properties. Currently not used in recipes, but planned for future alchemy and crafting systems.

- **✨ Amethyst Powder**  
  Finely crushed Amethyst shards. Used as a reagent in custom potion brewing.

- **⚪ Quartz Powder**  
  Pulverized Nether Quartz. Another key ingredient in custom alchemical recipes.

---

## 🧪 New Potion
- **🥤 Potion of Clarity**  
  Crafted using **Amethyst Powder** and **Quartz Powder**. This potion is designed to improve clarity of mind—intended for use in future gameplay mechanics or custom effects.

---

## 🔧 Features
- Adds crafting recipe support for items introduced via datapacks.
- Extends item functionality beyond what datapacks can natively offer.
- Modular codebase allows for quick additions of new items and recipes.
- Integrates seamlessly with vanilla crafting mechanics.

---

## ⚠ Requirements & Compatibility
- ✅ Requires **Fabric API**
- ✅ Compatible with **Minecraft 1.21.4**
- 🔄 **Must be installed on both client and server**
- ❌ Not compatible with Forge
